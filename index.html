A final, 10-day long celebration to Yang college towns in Iowa

Yang Party is assembling high energy humans to paint Iowa City red white and blue for Andrew Yang

Andrew is running a 21st century campaign, and this calls for 21st century canvassing. Knocking on doors is important work for reaching voters. However, many people find it unnatural. Making matters worse, some voters refuse to open their doors, and many college students live in dorms where we can't even knock.

Yang Party advocates a new form of canvassing, where

canvassers are paid a universal basic income of $33/day for each day they party with the group

. With these funds, we can spend time and money at local businesses in public spaces, where people are naturally more open to conversations. We can leverage the energy of the Yang Gang by lighting up coffee shops, restaurants, bars, yoga studios, college campuses and more. Instead of cold approaching Iowans at their homes, let them see how much fun we are and allow them to connect with us as humans first.
